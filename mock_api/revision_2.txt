Marketing Plan — NovaBrew Cold Coffee

Introduction

NovaBrew is launching a ready-to-drink cold brew line aimed at the mid-market segment. This plan sets out our positioning, target audience, and go-to-market approach for the first two quarters.

Market Analysis

The ready-to-drink coffee category has grown steadily among 18-34 year olds. Convenience and perceived quality are the two dominant purchase drivers in this segment.
