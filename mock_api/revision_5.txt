Marketing Plan — NovaBrew Cold Coffee

Introduction

NovaBrew is launching a ready-to-drink cold brew line aimed at the mid-market segment. This plan sets out our positioning, target audience, and go-to-market approach for the first two quarters.

Market Analysis

The ready-to-drink coffee category has grown steadily among 18-34 year olds. Convenience and perceived quality are the two dominant purchase drivers in this segment.

Positioning

NovaBrew positions between premium artisan brands and mass-market canned coffee. Our advantage is a genuinely cold-brewed product at a mid-market price, sold through convenience channels rather than specialty cafes. The messaging centers on "real cold brew, everyday price," which reframes the value comparison away from premium cafes and toward the canned incumbents whose taste quality is weaker.

Competitor Overview

The three main incumbents are BrewFast, ChillCan, and DarkRoast Go. BrewFast leads on distribution, ChillCan on price, and DarkRoast Go on brand. None currently offer a true cold-brew product at our price point.
